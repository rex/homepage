Regenerative Projects

Each initiative is a seed of a brighter tomorrow — designed with communities, powered by renewables, and shaped to restore ecological balance.

Community Microgrid

Sunweave Cooperative

A modular solar canopy system that shades public plazas while storing energy for neighborhood use. Built with biocomposite struts and citizen-maintained battery gardens.

Bio-Urbanism

Verdant Highline

An elevated mycelium walkway linking rooftops with edible gardens, misting stations, and pollinator wayfinding lights that respond to lunar cycles.

Sonic Ecology

Forest Resonance Pods

Immersive listening chambers tuned to the symphonies of nearby forests. Visitors experience live bioacoustics and contribute to open data for conservationists.

Circular Craft

Amberleaf Atelier

A repair-and-remix studio where neighbors learn biomaterial weaving, dyeing with algae pigments, and building zero-waste wearables.

Storytelling

Solar Scribe Network

An open storytelling platform archiving resilient practices through illustrated zines, localized broadcasts, and augmented reality park markers.

Climate Play

Bloom City Simulator

A cooperative tabletop and AR experience where players co-design regenerative districts, experiment with policy levers, and visualize carbon-positive futures.

About the Collective

We are a constellation of designers, energy stewards, farmers, storytellers, and dreamers. Each project is a collaborative tapestry woven from community wisdom, open-source tools, and respectful technology.

From solar-powered fabrication labs to community seed libraries, our practice centers on mutual aid and ecological restoration. We host workshops, artist residencies, and research sprints that help cities bloom into resilient bioregions.

Our studio is powered by 100% renewables, and we redistribute a portion of every commission to local climate justice funds. Join us in cultivating luminous futures.
